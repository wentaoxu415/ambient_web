Why Ambient AI?

In today's medicine, it seems like documentation comes before patients. According to a survey conducted by the Annals of Medicine, doctors spend 49% of time on handling paperwork, nearly twice as much time as seeing patients. We provide a mobile app that takes accurate medical notes based on voice so that practitioners can focus on what really matters.

How It Works

Using Ambient AI is as simple as 1-2-3
1. Dictate

Dictate to the phone, tablet, or watch and have notes appear in your EMR instantly.

2. Review

Review and edit your notes or submit to our specialized QA team for error checking.

3. Save

Sign off to save the final edition of your note.
